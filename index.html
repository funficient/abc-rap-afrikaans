Give underpriviledged youth the gift of language.

About

Children in unpriviledged communities have limited access to resources. In large classes they get no personal attention. At home their care givers often are addicts without the capacity to provide for their needs.

The result is children unable to read and write with no chance of breaking the cycle of poverty and getting even a low income job.
